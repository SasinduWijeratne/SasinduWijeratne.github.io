I am a Senior Engineer in Architecture at [Tenstorrent](https://www.tenstorrent.com/), working on performance modeling and workload analysis on RISC-V CPUs. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Engineering from the [University of Southern California](https://www.usc.edu/) in Summer 2025, advised by [Prof. Viktor Prasanna](https://viterbi-web3196.usc.edu/~viktorpr/). My thesis focused on hardware-aware algorithm design for sparse tensor decomposition across CPU, GPU, and FPGA.

My research interests include parallel and distributed computing, hardware–software co-design, computer architecture, tensor algebra and decomposition, and performance modeling.
